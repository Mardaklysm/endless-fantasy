The whole image is blocked by default. Walkable polygons paint playable paths and floors; blocker polygons cut objects out of them.

Choose Walkable or Blocker from the Draw menu. Left click adds polygon points. Click near the first point or press Enter to close. Escape cancels.

Shift + left click deletes the top zone under the cursor, preferring the selected draw layer.

Right drag creates an event rectangle. Right click edits an event. Shift + right click deletes an event.

Mouse wheel zooms. Middle drag or Space + drag pans.
